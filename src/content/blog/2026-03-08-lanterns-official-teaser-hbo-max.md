HBO Max has released the official teaser for Lanterns, the highly anticipated DC series based on the Green Lantern comics. The teaser dropped on March 4, 2026, and quickly shot to #2 on YouTube's Trending Movies chart, accumulating over 2.2 million views in just 3 days.

The series is set to premiere on HBO Max in August 2026. With 108K likes and strong early engagement, Lanterns appears to be generating significant buzz as one of the flagship DC Universe projects for HBO Max's 2026 lineup.
