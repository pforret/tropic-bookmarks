The Instagram account @booksandcoffeemx (Vero) ran a large-scale reader survey asking 60,000 followers to name the single book they always recommend to everyone. The results were compiled into a ranked top 100 list, posted as a multi-image carousel.

The post breaks down the findings into tiers: the most recommended book overall, runner-ups, books mentioned more than twice, and the favorite author across all responses. It's a crowdsourced snapshot of what avid readers consider essential reading.

With 15,000 likes and 761 comments, the post clearly struck a chord with the book community. It serves as a great discovery tool for readers looking for their next read, backed by the collective wisdom of tens of thousands of fellow bibliophiles.
