OpenGranola is an open-source meeting assistant for macOS built by @yazins. It transcribes both sides of a call entirely on-device — no audio ever leaves your Mac — and simultaneously searches a local folder of Markdown notes to surface relevant talking points exactly when the conversation needs them.

It works with any LLM via OpenRouter (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, Llama), is invisible to screen share, and requires no special setup beyond pointing it at your notes folder. The privacy-first, local-first design sets it apart from cloud-based meeting assistants.

With 260K views and strong early engagement, OpenGranola taps into real demand for AI meeting tools that don't require trusting a third-party service with sensitive conversation audio.
