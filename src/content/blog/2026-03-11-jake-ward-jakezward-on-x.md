Jake Ward shares a detailed breakdown of a programmatic SEO experiment that generated 13,000+ pages in under 3 hours, growing weekly organic clicks from 971 to 5,500 — a 466% increase in 60 days, with roughly half the pages not yet indexed.

The key insight: instead of writing content page by page, the system treats content like software. AI never writes freeform — it fills strict JSON schemas with niche-aware data across 309 niches and 6 content categories (resource pages, comparison pages, free tools, and more). Gemini Flash populates type-safe JSON files that feed 20+ rendering templates.

Ward calls this 'Programmatic SEO 2.0' — no template spam, no thin city-page swaps, but genuinely useful pages built at scale. The approach reframes pSEO from a content hack into a content engineering discipline, and the early traffic results suggest the curve is still climbing.
