The Claws Belgian User Group held its second meetup at Betacowork in Brussels on March 5, 2026, bringing together OpenClaw enthusiasts for an evening of networking, demos, and shared lessons learned.

The free event ran from 6 PM to 9 PM, with pizza included and an optional €5-10 contribution. Attendees ranged from newcomers to seasoned builders, all gathered around the shared interest of building AI assistants on the OpenClaw platform.

The positive reception of the first Betacowork edition clearly carried momentum into this second gathering, signaling a growing local community around open, agentic AI infrastructure.
